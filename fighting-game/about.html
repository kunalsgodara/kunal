About GAME

It is a multiplayer fighter game that lets two players fight with each other.
player one has 'W' , 'A' , 'D' , 'SPACE' for jump ,left, right, ATTACK movement respectively.
player two has 'UP' , 'LEFT' , 'RIGHT' , 'DOWN' for jump ,left, right, ATTACK movement respectively.
The player who's health finishes earlier dies and the other player wins.
